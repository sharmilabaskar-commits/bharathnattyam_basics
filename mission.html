My Mission

🌟 Core Mission

To make classical Bharatanatyam dance accessible to underprivileged children and communities.

📚 Teaching Basics to Underprivileged Kids

I am committed to providing free or affordable Bharatanatyam lessons. Through my teaching, I aim to:

  • Introduce them to classical Indian dance and cultural heritage
  • Build confidence, discipline, and self-expression
  • Create opportunities for them to perform and showcase talent
  • Inspire the next generation of classical dancers

  • Initiative in teaching and creating impact
  • Digital skills and personal branding
